Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Spotter is designed for privacy-conscious wildlife exploration. The app does its best to run classification locally on your device, keep location and media data under your control, and avoid unnecessary sharing.

What we collect

  • Photos and observations you choose to save.
  • App settings and observation metadata (timestamps, labels, and notes).
  • Optional bug reports and diagnostics when you explicitly enable them.

How we use it

  • To power your local observation timeline and notes.
  • To improve future classification quality and app experience.
  • To answer support requests when you contact us.

Your rights

You can delete saved observations at any time. You can disable permissions in device settings and request support for data removal.

Terms of Service

Last updated: February 25, 2026

By using Spotter, you agree to use the app responsibly while visiting zoos and to follow all posted facility rules and conservation best practices.

User responsibilities

  • Use the app respectfully and never disturb animals or staff.
  • Do not misuse the app to upload harmful or illegal content.
  • Follow local laws, permit rules, and museum or zoo policies.

Limitations

On-device classification results are suggestions and may include errors. Spotter is not liable for misidentifications, lost data from device issues, or incidents that occur during your real-world visits.
