Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Spotter is designed for privacy-conscious wildlife exploration. The app does its best to run classification locally on your device, keep location and media data under your control, and avoid unnecessary sharing.

What we collect

  • Photos and observations you choose to save.
  • App settings and observation metadata (such as timestamps and labels).
  • Optional bug reports and crash diagnostics if enabled in settings.

How we use it

  • To power your local observation timeline and notes.
  • To improve image classification performance in future app versions.
  • To offer support if you contact us.

How we protect it

Data is encrypted when available, minimized where possible, and never sold. We only share information with partners you explicitly authorize.

Your rights

You can delete saved observations at any time. You can also remove app permissions and request support for deletion of account-related data through in-app support.
